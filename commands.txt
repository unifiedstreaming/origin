[Activate an existing plugin/use case (DASH)]

  Uncomment /etc/manifest-edit/conf/mpd/default.yaml
  
  OR

  Drop a yaml:

  cp /usr/share/manifest-edit/mpd/label_add.yaml /etc/manifest-edit/conf/mpd/

  Get a manifest and save it: 
  
    curl "http://localhost/usp-s3-storage/tears-of-steel/tears-of-steel-wvtt.ism/.mpd" --output tos_original.mpd

  Get the edited manifest and save it: 
  
    curl "http://localhost/usp-s3-storage/tears-of-steel/tears-of-steel-wvtt.ism/.mpd?suppress_query_parameters=true&python_pipeline_config=mpd/label_add" --output tos_edited.mpd
